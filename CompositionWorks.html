String Quartets

Iridescent River(2020)

d: 9:00

"Iridescent River" for string quartet is a three-movement composition that immerses listeners in the varied energies and moods of a vibrant watercourse. Through the expressive capabilities of the string quartet, each movement encapsulates a distinct facet of the river's character, offering a nuanced musical exploration of the lively rapids, tranquil stream, and majestic falls.

Movement I: Rapids

The opening movement, "Rapids," sweeps listeners into the tumultuous heart of the river as interpreted by a string quartet. This lively movement captures the untamed energy of rushing waters through dynamic bowing, intricate pizzicato passages, and vigorous interplay between the instruments. The strings emulate the spirited turbulence of rapids, portraying the vivid and unpredictable journey of the iridescent river as it navigates through rocky landscapes.

Movement II: Stream

Transitioning to a more tranquil atmosphere, the second movement, "Stream," flows gently with the grace of a string quartet. Delicate melodies and legato phrasing mirror the serene beauty of a calm river stream. The quartet's strings intertwine seamlessly, creating a sense of tranquility and reflection. "Stream" invites listeners to peacefully drift along the iridescent river's more placid stretches, where the current whispers tales of serenity and the beauty of nature's quietude.

Movement III: Falls

The final movement, "Falls," marks the grand culmination of the iridescent river's journey, as portrayed by the expressive voices of a string quartet. Majestic and powerful, this movement emulates the breathtaking descent of a waterfall through dramatic bowing, intense tremolos, and resonant harmonics. The quartet builds a crescendo, capturing the anticipation before plunging into a tumultuous cascade of sound. The strings vividly portray the sheer force and awe-inspiring beauty of the falls. As the river concludes its tumultuous descent, the quartet resolves with a sense of grandeur, leaving listeners in awe of the iridescent river's majestic journey from rapids to stream and, finally, to the breathtaking falls.

Performance by: Mable Lecrone (Violin), Aadhi Ramkumar (Violin), Celest Meisel (Viola), Jessica Barrick (Cello)
